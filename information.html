DIRECTIONS

In order to find the Longwood Lions Arena, please enter the Longwood High School building from the Visitors entrance, and walk straight. The arena will be to your right.


POLICIES

Event Cancellation Policy: In the event of an event cancellation, ticket holders will be offered a full refund or the option to transfer tickets to a rescheduled date, depending on the circumstances.


Food and Beverage Policy: Guests are allowed to bring in pre-packaged snacks, but all outside beverages, including water, must be purchased on-site to maintain arena security and revenue streams.


Code of Conduct for Staff: All arena staff are required to adhere to a strict code of conduct, ensuring professionalism, respect, and proper customer service at all times.


Lost and Found Policy: The arena is not responsible for personal items left behind, but lost items will be securely stored for a maximum of 30 days for retrieval.


Health & Safety Protocols: All guests must comply with health and safety measures, including clear bag policies, temperature checks, or mask mandates, as required by local regulations.


AMENITIES

There are 3 bathrooms available at the Longwood Lions Arena. There is 1 female, 1 male, and 1 non-gender conforming/family bathroom. Food is not provided.
